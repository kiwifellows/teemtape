teemtape is an open-source **ticker app for commenting on stocks**. You build a
watchlist of symbols, see delayed quotes, and leave **anonymous notes** that are
shared by everyone with the watchlist link — whether they post from the web app,
the CLI, or an AI agent.

:::caution[Not a trading app]
There is no order entry, portfolio, or money movement. Quotes are delayed by
about a minute and are **informational only**.
:::
